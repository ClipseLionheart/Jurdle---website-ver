How to Play

Jurdle is a fun game where you try to guess a hidden word in six guesses or less! Use your keyboard to type, and refresh to replay!

Rules

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. If it is not, the blank spaces will show up as yellow.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • If the letter is in the word and in the correct spot, it will turn green.
  • If the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, it will turn yellow.
  • If the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, it will turn white.
